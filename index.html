Our Mission

A Sustainable Archive of Black Excellence in Medicine

Black Men in Medicine exists to build and maintain a living archive that documents, celebrates, and preserves Black excellence across the physician pipeline.

This is not a one-time snapshot. The goal is a record that expands with every match cycle, across every major specialty, so that progress remains visible, citable, and difficult to ignore.

The archive is meant to carry both recognition and historical weight: it honors the people moving medicine forward while preserving a public record that can be referenced over time.

Representation matters. A permanent record matters too.
